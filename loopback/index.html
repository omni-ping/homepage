Talk to it. It remembers.

Prism is the AI agent at the center of the app. Tell it what's on your mind, by text or voice, and it builds threads, sets reminders, reads your calendar, and searches across everything you've saved. Voice goes in through Whisper. Everything syncs through PowerSync and Supabase, offline-first, so it works on the subway.

A thread is a topic, a task, anything you're tracking. Pin it, snooze it, complete it. Or just ask Prism.

  • Voice capture via Whisper
  • Google Calendar + Gmail: Prism knows what's on your plate
  • Push notifications, timezone-aware scheduling
  • Offline-first — works without signal, syncs when you're back
  • Runs Claude Sonnet 4.6

Coming soon: scheduled prompts. "Every Monday, review my overdue threads."
